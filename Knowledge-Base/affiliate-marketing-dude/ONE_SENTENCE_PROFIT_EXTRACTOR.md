# ONE-SENTENCE PROFIT EXTRACTOR
## Marcus Campbell Method

**Core Principle (Gary Halbert):** People buy because of ONE little point that hits them where they live.

---

## The 4-Step System

### Step 1: Offer Ingestion
Feed AI the complete sales page, video script, testimonials, FAQs, bonuses.

### Step 2: Sentence Extraction
Extract 20 raw emotional sentences categorized:
- Pain sentences
- Desire sentences
- Mechanism sentences
- Fear sentences
- Scarcity sentences
- Identity shifts
- Cost of inaction

### Step 3: Angle Breakdown
Match each sentence to a different buyer persona.

### Step 4: Traffic Deployment
Create content cluster for each sentence:
- 1 sentence = 1 SEO post
- 1 sentence = 1 video hook
- 1 sentence = 1 Pinterest pin
- 1 sentence = 1 tool/calculator/quiz

---

## EXAMPLE: Save the Marriage Offer

**Sentence:** "Couples spend average less than 4 minutes talking about real issues"

**Content outputs:**
- Blog: "15-Minute Marriage Reset"
- Tool: Marriage Decay Calculator
- Quiz: "Roommate or Lovers?"
- Worksheet: "5 Questions That Take 4+ Minutes"
- Video: "They were ready to end their 14-year marriage. This ONE thing changed everything"

---

## Prompt

Extract 20 raw emotional sentences from this offer.
Categorize by: Pain, Desire, Mechanism, Fear, Identity, Scarcity.
Output 10 SEO titles, 5 calculator ideas, 10 Pinterest pins.

---

## Rules

1. Promote the psychological engine INSIDE the product
2. Each sentence = different buyer persona
3. Build UTILITIES not just content
4. Work backwards from what pays
5. Stay surface level unless expert

---

## Bottom Line

Marcus made $127,000 from ONE offer by extracting ONE sentence per buyer persona and building content around each.

Smart affiliates don't promote products. They promote the ONE SENTENCE inside that makes money.
