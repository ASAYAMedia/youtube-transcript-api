## 🔥 How This Connects EVERYTHING

### Intent + Domains
When buying domains, ask:
- What would someone search to find this?
- What's the intent?
- What offer matches?

**Example:**
- Domain: GasSavingsCalculator.com
- Intent: Save money on gas
- Offer: Gas credit card ($100/lead)

---

### Intent + AdSense
AdSense works because:
- Ads match user intent
- Higher intent = higher CPC
- Target "problem" and "transactional" keywords

---

### Intent + Affiliate
Affiliate conversion depends on:
- Matching offer to intent
- Not just "recommending" but "solving"

**Example flows:**
```
"mortgage calculator" → calculate mortgage → offer: refinance loan
"best credit card" → compare cards → offer: Capital One ($200/lead)
"how to fix credit" → repair guide → offer: credit repair service
```
