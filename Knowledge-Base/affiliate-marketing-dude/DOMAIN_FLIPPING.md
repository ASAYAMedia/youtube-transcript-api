# Domain Flipping Masterclass

## 🔥 1. BULLETPROOF OUTLINE

### Core Concept: What Domain Flipping Is

- Buy domain cheap → sell higher
- Like digital real estate (but more calculated)

**NOT:**
- get-rich-quick

**IS:**
- probability-based business

---

### The 2 Ways Domains Make Money

#### 1. SEO Value
- Backlinks
- Rankings
- Traffic
- Past authority

**Used for:**
- Building websites
- Reselling to SEOs

#### 2. Name Value (MOST IMPORTANT)
- Short
- Memorable
- Keyword-rich
- Brandable

**Example:**
crypto.com = valuable because of demand

---

### Where to Find Domains

**Expired domains:**
- expireddomains.net
- SpamZilla
- GoDaddy auctions

**New domains:**
- Creative naming
- Trend-based ideas

---

### What Makes a Domain Valuable

**Checklist:**
- Short & clean
- Easy to remember
- Has demand (finance, fitness, etc.)
- No spam history
- No trademark issues
- Has backlinks OR strong name

---

### The Buying Strategy

**Low Risk Approach**
- Buy $8–$10 domains
- Sell for:
  - $10–$100 (quick flips)
  - $100–$1000+ (good names)

**Key insight:**
If you can recover your $9 → risk = near zero

---

### The Real Game: Probability

- Buy many GOOD domains
- Not random junk
- Play odds, not luck

---

### How to Evaluate Domains

**Step-by-step:**
1. Check backlinks (Ahrefs)
2. Check rankings (keywords)
3. Check spam history
4. Check brandability
5. Check appraisal (GoDaddy / Estibot)

**Ask:**
"Can I resell this easily?"

---

### Pricing Strategy

- Base price = what you'd accept
- Compare:
  - GoDaddy appraisal
  - Estibot

**Final truth:**
worth = what buyer pays

---

### Where to Sell

- GoDaddy Auctions
- Sedo
- Afternic
- Direct sales
- Your own site

---

### Aggressive Selling (BIG MONEY)

**Methods:**
- Contact businesses directly
- Find similar domains owners
- Offer upgrades
- Cold email buyers

---

### Hidden Strategy (VERY IMPORTANT)

**"Free Website Hack"**

Give domain FREE
Make money from:
- hosting affiliate ($100+)
- upsells
- Client wins → you win

---

### Risk Management

**ALWAYS ask:**
- Can I get my money back?
- Worst-case scenario?

**If YES → buy**
**If NO → skip**

---

### Domain Flipping vs Website Flipping

| | Domain Flipping | Website Flipping |
|--|----------------|------------------|
| What you sell | Name only | Built site |
| Effort | Low | High |
| Margin | $20-200+ | $500-5000+ |

## 🧠 2. STUDY NOTES (DEEP UNDERSTANDING)

### 1. This is NOT Luck — It's Pattern Recognition

You're not guessing. You're:
- spotting demand
- spotting keywords
- spotting buyers

### 2. Beginners Lose Money Because

they buy random domains
they ignore demand
they don't think about resale

**Rule:**
"If you don't know who will buy it → don't buy it."

### 3. The REAL Skill = Buyer Thinking

Before buying ask:
- Who wants this?
- Why would they pay?
- Can I reach them?

### 4. Two Winning Plays

**Safe Play:**
- Buy cheap ($8)
- Flip small ($20–$100)

**Big Play:**
- Find high-demand keyword
- Hold → sell for $1k–$10k+

### 5. Backlinks = Hidden Gold

Domains with authority = faster SEO
Businesses PAY for this

### 6. Brandable Domains = Lottery Tickets

- No traffic
- No backlinks

BUT:
right buyer = huge payout

### 7. The Hybrid Strategy (BEST)

Combine:
- Domain flipping
- Website building
- Affiliate income

**How pros make consistent money**

### 8. Time Factor

Some domains sell:
- instantly

Some take:
- months / years

### 9. Ego Kills Profits

Don't assume value
Market decides

### 10. The Biggest Insight

"You don't need to be right every time — just profitable overall."
