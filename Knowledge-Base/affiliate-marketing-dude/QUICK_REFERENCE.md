**The missing link that connects everything.**

Before you build anything, you must understand this: **People search with PURPOSE.** Every keyword = a problem or desire.

**The Money Flow:**
```
KEYWORD → INTENT → CONTENT → OFFER → MONEY
```

**Intent = Shortcut to money.**

```
USER → SEARCHES ON GOOGLE → FINDS YOUR SITE → CLICKS AD → ADVERTISER PAYS GOOGLE → GOOGLE PAYS YOU
```

**Example:**
- User searches "best credit card"

**You're NOT competing with Google**
**You're PART of their ecosystem**

Google doesn't want to lose traffic → They pay you to bring it back through AdSense

**Prompt:**
> Extract 20 raw emotional sentences from this sales page that explain WHY people buy. Categorize: Pain, Desire, Mechanism, Fear, Identity, Scarcity. Output 10 SEO titles, 5 tool ideas, 10 Pinterest pins.

**Key insight:** Smart affiliates don't promote products — they promote the ONE SENTENCE inside that makes money.

### Domain Flipping (Digital Real Estate)

**Core concept:** Buy domains cheap ($8-10) → sell higher ($20-200+) → repeat

**Two ways domains make money:**

1. **SEO Value** — existing backlinks, rankings, traffic
   - Fast authority for building sites
   - Resell to SEO agencies

2. **Name Value (MOST IMPORTANT)** — short, memorable, keyword-rich
   - Example: crypto.com = millions because of demand

**Where to find domains:**
- expireddomains.net (free research)
- SpamZilla (check spam history)
- GoDaddy Auctions
- Manual creative naming

**The Hidden Strategy (VERY IMPORTANT):**
Give domain FREE to local business → make money from:
- Hosting affiliate commission ($100-$200+)
- Website setup service ($500-$2000+)
- Upsells (logo, branding, marketing)

**Risk Management:**
- Always ask: "Can I get my money back? Can I sell fast?"
- Golden Rule: **"If I can't sell it, can I still use it?"**

**Safe Play vs Big Play:**
| Approach | Buy | Sell |
|----------|-----|------|
| Safe | $8-10 | $20-100 (quick flips) |
| Big | $8-10 | $1k-10k+ (hold for right buyer) |

**Hybrid Strategy (BEST):**
Domain flipping + Website building + Affiliate = massive value creation

**Tools:**
- expireddomains.net (domain research)
- Ahrefs (backlinks check)
- GoDaddy/Afternic (sales platforms)
- Cold email (direct outreach to buyers)

### AdSense vs Affiliate: The Hybrid Model

**The shocking truth:** Same traffic:
- AdSense = $12/day
- Affiliate = $4,500/day

**Why the gap?**
- AdSense pays for attention (clicks/impressions)
- Affiliate pays for conversions (sales/leads)

**The Intent Ladder:**
- **Curiosity stage** → AdSense works best (researchers, browsers)
- **Buying stage** → Affiliate works best (ready to purchase)

**The Hybrid Strategy (MOST PROFITABLE):**
1. **AdSense** = baseline income from non-buyers (floor)
2. **Affiliate links** = high income from buyer-intent traffic (ceiling)
3. **Email list** = long-term asset for repeat sales

**What determines income:**
1. Niche (99% of income) — finance, credit, home = high CPC
2. Intent quality — targeted > random traffic
3. Time on site — more pages = more ads = more money

**Best content types:**
- Calculators & tools (mortgage, BMI, converters)
- Comparison guides
- Problem explanations
- Glossaries

**Traffic multiplication:**
- 1 visitor × 10 pages = 30 ad impressions
- Tools keep people longer = more revenue

**Golden rules:**
- Topic > effort (pick high-paying niches)
- Intent > traffic (quality over quantity)
- Strategy > tools (what you make matters more than how)
- AdSense = floor income, not ceiling
